Y9Societal Excursion

Area of Focus

Exploring Culture

Explanation

In this expedition, Year 9 students will be visiting sites in Malaysia which have historic and cultural significance. Students will have the opportunity to explore the history of the places they visit, as well as understand the efforts to protect heritage. This program aims to foster a deeper understanding and appreciation for our host country (Malaysia), as well as give students a glimpse of the complexities, challenges, and beauty of a modern & diverse society like Malaysia.
Where
Malacca, Kuala Lumpur, etc.
When
May / April Example Content Nature & City Tours, Shows & Presentations, Games & Challenges, Cultural Lessons, End-of-Day Reflection Activities Example Output Tourism Campaign Commercial (Group) - Students are to produce a short tourism campaign video for the site they visited, highlighting things they saw, ate, did, etc. Theme and tone of the videos are left to the creative discretion of the students.
